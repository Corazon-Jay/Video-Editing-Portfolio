About

The editor behind
the work

I'm Jared Ogboriefor — a freelance video editor based in Benin City, Nigeria, with over five years of experience turning raw footage into purposeful stories. My work spans ministry films, church event promos, and choir productions.

I've led the media department at Ministry of Perfection Church Inc., overseeing videography, broadcast, display, photography, and content units. That means I don't just edit — I understand production from the ground up and can collaborate at any stage of a project.

Whether it's a 60-second brand ad or a full-length documentary, I bring the same attention to pace, colour, and emotion to every cut.
